About Me

Analytical and proactive engineering professional with over 9 years of experience in electronics manufacturing, specializing in failure analysis, process improvement, and quality systems. My hands-on background spans from production floor operations to leading complex failure investigations, giving me a comprehensive understanding of electronic systems, test processes, and quality dynamics.

Currently working as a Failure Analysis Engineer at RAKON Limited, where I lead root cause investigations, streamline quality workflows, and deploy data visualizations to drive continuous improvement. My background includes extensive hands-on work with electronic testing, quality standards like ISO 9001 and IATF 16949, and programming for automation and data analysis.

Academically, I hold a Master of Computer and Information Sciences with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence & Knowledge Engineering (in progress) and a Bachelor of Engineering Technology in Electrical Engineering, both from Auckland University of Technology.

I enjoy working where electronics, data, and innovation intersect — and I’m always eager to learn, automate, and solve meaningful problems.
